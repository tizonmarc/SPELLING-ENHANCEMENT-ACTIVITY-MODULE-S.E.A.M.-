Spelling Lesson 2: The Many Jobs of Silent




Converting the preceding vowel to long sound is onlyoneof the
important duties the Silent E performs. The versatile and multi-
tasking Silent E actually has seven different jobs.

    1 Job#1: Silent E makes the vowel sound before it long,as in plate and wrote.
    2 Job#2: Silent E can make c and g soft,as in dance and large.
    3 Job#3: Silent E keeps i,u, and v from being the letter in a word, as in pie,true and give.
    4 Job#4: Silent E can show that the word is not plural, as in dense,tease and lapse (which would otherwise look like dense,tease and lapse).
    5 Job#5: Silent E adds a vowel to syllabus with the consonant +l - e syllable pattern,as in bundle and puzzle.
    6 Job#6: Silent E makes th say its second sound, /th/as in breathe and bathe.
    7 Job#7: Silent E clarifies the meaning of the word, in the word pairs b bye and aw awe.
