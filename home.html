INTRODUCTION

Spelling Enhancement Actitivty Module (S.E.A.M.) in ENGLISH is a collection of exercises designed for the following purposes: a) to improve the spelling competency of the students through strict observation and practice of the basic guidelines in spelling out new words and b) to encourage the habit of reading more books/articles on the part of the students.
